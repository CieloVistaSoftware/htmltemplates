Master Grade Top Woods

Sitka Spruce (AAA Grade)

Origin: Pacific Northwest, Canada

Our highest grade Sitka spruce features exceptional stiffness-to-weight ratio, providing powerful projection with pristine clarity. Characterized by tight, straight grain patterns and optimal density.

Western Red Cedar (Master Grade)

Origin: British Columbia, Canada

Selected for its warm, rich overtones and quick response. Perfect for fingerstyle players, our master grade cedar tops offer exceptional cross-grain stiffness and distinctive reddish-brown hues.

Exhibition Grade Tonewoods

Brazilian Rosewood (Exhibition Grade)

Origin: Sustainably Sourced, Pre-CITES Stock

The king of tonewoods, our Brazilian rosewood sets feature spectacular figure and color variation. Known for complex harmonics, deep bass response, and crystalline trebles.

Madagascar Rosewood (Premium Grade)

Origin: Madagascar (Ethically Sourced)

Selected for its similarity to Brazilian rosewood, offering rich, projective tone with excellent separation between notes. Features striking dark chocolate to violet hues.

Origin: Central America

Prized for its exceptional stability and focused midrange response. Our select grade sets feature quarter-sawn orientation for maximum stability and tonal projection.

Australian Blackwood (Premium Grade)

Origin: Tasmania, Australia

A sustainable alternative to koa, featuring similar tonal properties with distinctive golden-brown coloring and complex figure patterns.
