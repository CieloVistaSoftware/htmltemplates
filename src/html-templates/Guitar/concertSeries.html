Concert Series

Where Balance Meets Brilliance

Our Concert Series guitars deliver the perfect balance of comfort, projection, and tonal clarity. Designed for the discerning musician who demands versatility without compromise.

The Concert Difference

The concert body size represents the sweet spot in acoustic guitar design – large enough for substantial volume and bass response, yet comfortable enough for extended playing sessions. Our concert guitars feature a slightly smaller waist than dreadnoughts, creating a more focused midrange that cuts through any mix.

Each Concert Series guitar is meticulously crafted using traditional techniques passed down through generations of luthiers. We select only the finest tonewoods, aged to perfection, ensuring each instrument develops its own unique voice over time.

  • Hand-selected premium tonewoods
  • Traditional hide glue construction
  • Hand-fitted dovetail neck joint
  • Bone nut and saddle
