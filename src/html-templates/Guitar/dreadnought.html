Dreadnought

The King of Acoustic Guitars

Born from the legendary Martin D-28 tradition, our Dreadnought series delivers the iconic sound that defined acoustic music. Bold, powerful, and unmistakably American in character.

Legendary Power

The Dreadnought body shape revolutionized acoustic guitar design in the 1930s, and its influence continues to shape music today. With its large body cavity and distinctive waisted figure-8 shape, the Dreadnought produces unmatched volume and bass response.

Our Dreadnought series honors this legacy while incorporating modern innovations. Each guitar features carefully graduated tops, scalloped X-bracing, and premium appointments that enhance both tone and playability. Whether you're strumming around a campfire or recording in Nashville, these guitars deliver the authentic Dreadnought experience.

  • Forward-shifted X-bracing pattern
  • Hand-scalloped braces for optimal resonance
  • Vintage-style slotted headstock option
  • High-quality Grover tuning machines
